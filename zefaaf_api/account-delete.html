Zefaaf - Account Deletion Request

To request the deletion of your account, please follow these steps:

  1. Log in to your account.
  2. Navigate to the Account Settings page.
  3. Click on the "Delete Account" option.
  4. Follow the on-screen instructions to confirm the deletion.

The following types of data will be deleted:

  • User profile information
  • Account preferences
  • Usage history

Any personal data will be retained for 30 days in case of accidental deletion.
